Since 2005, Zephaniah has been involved in a growing revival of power and thrash metal in the American Midwest. Initially recognized for an energetic stage show and technically challenging music, the release of their first full album, Stories from the Book of Metal (2008), helped gain the group recognition in the global underground metal scene, independently selling approximately 850 copies by October 2010.


Zephaniah's new release - REFORGED (2015) - features eight new songs showcasing the members' talents. Listeners can expect memorable melodies and must-hear musicality, tightly produced and delivered with a modern approach to the traditional power/thrash format. As supplemental material, the band has also rerecorded two songs from the Stories from the Book of Metal album, which may be released as promotional tracks or album bonus tracks.


On and off stage, Zephaniah has worked hard to network and become a recognizable name in the tight-knit metal community. With tour dates from Kansas City to New York City and performances alongside notable bands such as Aska, Raven, and Michael Angelo Batio, Zephaniah is aiming for wider distribution and more touring opportunities with the upcoming release of REFORGED.
